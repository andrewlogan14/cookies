Happiness is our main ingredient
Learn more about where our famous recipes come from!

The amazing Granny B's Cookies lineup is about baking happiness. Beginning with Grandma Blackett's first frosted cookies more than 50 years ago, Granny B's baked goods are made to celebrate and to enjoy. Smiles of satisfaction are the hallmark for all of our offerings. We are in the business of baking, packaging and delivering little bundles of sweetness to you.

Today, Granny B's has hundreds of retailers and distributors who we partner with to bring our delicious cookies to parties, special events, schools and kitchen tables throughout the United States, Canada, and Mexico.

We have expanded our line of cookies and bakery treats to include our signature frosted sugar cookies, soft wire-cut cookies, buttery shortbread cookies, brownies, cornbread, COKOS, and new Crispy Butter cookies. These baked goods come in a variety of retail ready packaging, including our famous individually-wrapped cookies, the traditional clamshells, giftable boxes, as well as bulk foodservice options. We have also recently become a full OU Kosher facility.

Granny B's has the breadth of products to meet your everyday and seasonal needs and the homemade goodness that will surprise and delight your customers. We bake and decorate attractive cookies that are fresh and delicious, bringing you back again... and again... and again. Enjoy eating Granny B's.
